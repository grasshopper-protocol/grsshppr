# LinkedIn Post — Version 1: Narrative Hook

I spent a weekend building an open-source mentoring platform with Claude.

Not *using* Claude. Building *with* Claude.

Grasshopper is now in beta — a free, goal-based mentoring platform for tech & design professionals. No paywalls. No marketplace fees. Just intentional mentoring relationships.

The most surprising part? Working with AI didn't make me faster. It made me clearer.

Every feature forced me to articulate *why* it should exist. Every architecture decision became a conversation. Claude remembered past decisions so I didn't have to re-explain context.

This is what I've been writing about for two years: augmented humans. Designers, engineers, makers who use AI to amplify their work, not replace it.

Grasshopper is proof that it works.

We're looking for contributors (engineers, designers, PMs) and mentors (3+ years experience). Everything is public: code, roadmap, decisions.
